The Approach

Four Chapters, One Story

Every project starts with a Face to Space consultation and follows a chapter-based journey guided by our signature method: Review. Release. Reclaim. Donate.

Chapter 1
Story Unlocked
Discovery and assessment. We learn your space, your story, and your goals.
Chapter 2
Plan of Purpose
Your personalized roadmap with room-by-room direction and sourcing strategy.
Chapter 3
Design in Motion
Hands-on execution. Sorting, styling, sourcing, and transforming your space.
Chapter 4
The Reveal
Your finished space, complete with systems to sustain the transformation.
