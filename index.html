About

Meet Nichole

Originally from New Jersey, Nichole's passion for design and visual conception was inspired by her late grandfather, a carpenter who taught her that every space tells a story. At 14, she discovered her calling: organizing and designing to help others reach their inner potential.

With a BS in Interior Design from The Art Institute of Philadelphia and over a decade of professional experience spanning design, sales operations, and strategic leadership, Nichole brings a unique blend of creative vision and operational precision to everything she touches.

"The presentation of your home is an extension of who you are. Every space deserves to be designed with intention."

IWP was born from a belief that intentionality should extend beyond your home - into your career, your celebrations, and every moment that matters.
